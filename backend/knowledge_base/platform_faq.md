# Platform Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is AgriConnect AI?
AgriConnect AI is a smart, direct-to-consumer digital marketplace built specifically for agricultural stakeholders. It bridges the gap between rural farmers, equipment owners, and commercial buyers by facilitating local product listings, secure interactions, and peer-to-peer equipment renting.

## Product Selling
Farmers and agricultural sellers can register on the platform and publish crop or product listings for free. By submitting details like crop type, harvest volume, base pricing, and pickup district, sellers make their produce instantly visible to prospective buyers browsing the marketplace.

## Product Buying
Buyers can search the marketplace catalog, filter by product category, filter by target district, or apply price limits to find appropriate farm listings. When interested, they click "View Details" to see the seller information.

## Verified Sellers
To build transactional trust, AgriConnect AI offers seller verification. Sellers who complete basic identity and location check validations receive a visual confirmation badge on their listings, signifying a lower probability of service disruption or misrepresentation.

## Contacting Sellers
Buyers can view verified contact details, such as phone numbers and names, listed directly on the product's details panel. AgriConnect AI recommends that buyers confirm availability and logistics before initiating shipments or payments.

## Reporting Suspicious Listings
If users encounter listings with fraudulent details, misleading prices, duplicate records, or banned goods, they should flag the listing via the "Report Listing" system. AgriConnect moderation personnel review reported entries within 24 hours to enforce policy compliance.

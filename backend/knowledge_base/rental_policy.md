# Equipment Rental Policy

## Rental Flow

### 1. Searching for Equipment
Renters can search for agricultural machinery (such as tractors, crop sprayers, harvesters, rotavators, and seeders) filtering by location, price, and category.

### 2. Checking Availability
Each equipment detail page displays a real-time calendar indicating available rental slots updated by the equipment owner.

### 3. Submitting a Request
The renter selects the desired dates and submits an equipment booking request. The owner is automatically notified via their registered contact phone/email.

### 4. Booking Confirmation
The equipment owner has 48 hours to approve or decline the booking request. Once confirmed, contact coordinates are shared between the two parties.

### 5. Pickup & Return
Both parties must inspect the machinery together upon pickup and return to document its operating condition. Renter must return the equipment on the designated end date in clean, working condition.

### 6. Cancellation Rules
Renters can cancel requests penalty-free up to 24 hours prior to the pickup time. Cancellations made inside the 24-hour window may incur a standard convenience fee.

### 7. Security Deposit
Depending on the machinery type, equipment owners may request a security deposit. The security deposit is fully refunded within 24 hours of returning the machinery safely.

## Important Disclaimer
AgriConnect AI provides directory coordination only. Final equipment rental terms, usage parameters, mechanical insurance liability, and physical logistics are agreed upon directly between the equipment owner and the renter.
